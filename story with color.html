STORY WITH COLOR

2019

Using the emotions and memories depicted in the photo of me when I was a baby, I created an abstract composition using only colors. The story takes place in Hawaii my parents were cleaning the bed sheets and me doodling inside the drawer. The youthful and playful tone is the dominant element in the design. Play Doh looking shapes really bring back my childhood.

Risograph Printer was used adding the dusty, grainy effect to 11 by 17 prints.
