ABOUT WORK

Story with Color

Color, Risograph

2019

Using the emotions and memories captured in the photo of me when I was a baby, I created an abstract composition using only colors. The story takes place in Hawaii when my parents were cleaning the bed sheets and me doodling inside the drawer. The naiveness and playful tone of me as a child are the dominant elements in the design. Play-Doh looking shapes really bring back my childhood.
