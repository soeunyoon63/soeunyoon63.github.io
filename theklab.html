TheKlab

Objective

Enables salespeople to connect remotely with clients and enhances networking through data collection

Role

Prototyping, UI Design, UX Design

Tools

Figma, Zeplin, Cinema 4D

Overview

In this role, I worked as a UI/UX designer in the Summer 2020 and continued the work remotely.

Role

My roles included creating platforms for TheKlab website and mobile application, and infographic booklet for this application. Additionally, I conducted preliminary research to support the intervention and UI decisions such as user flow, microinteractions, and prototyping. I ensured that the interface provides a seamless experience to all target users.

TheKlab

TheKlab specializes in B2B sales/marketing consulting. The company studies knowledge curation and data information to provide a strong networking, easy accessibility for individuals within the department, and opportunity for new knowledge through connections.

Problem

Due to the changes such as contact-free and social distancing regulations in the business workplace, the business method must change from the past.

Deliverable

How can I create design solutions for changes in the operation strategy such as limited communication with clients and the lack of motivation in sales people due to working from home?

Persona

Preliminary research and insights helped to create personas.

User Journey

Considering the user's goals and process help to define a proper accessibility to the website and the application.
