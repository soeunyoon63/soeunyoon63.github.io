Overview

Witnessing ongoing environmental changes inspired me to convey that living minimally not only benefits the environment but can also be embraced as a stylistic movement. Each page represents the principles of deduction, substitution, and redaction. I chose recycled newsprint to physically embody this message and emphasize its environmental impact.
