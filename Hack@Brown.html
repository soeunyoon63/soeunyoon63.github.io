Brand Identity, Motion

Hack@Brown is an annual hackathon run by students at Brown University. Working with non-designers and designers, branding for Hack@Brown 2020 is created under this year theme, Garage.

Goal

As coding seems to target specific group of people and represents a stereotypical image, a more approachable and friendlier brand identity is created with colors that throw us back to childhood. The 3D assists are some of the objects that are found in a garage.
