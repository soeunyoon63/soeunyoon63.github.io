Overview

In this role, I worked as a brand designer with UI/UX principles in mind.
Hack@Brown’s mission is to empower everyone to learn and build awesome projects at annual hackathons. Driven by the core ideals of diversity and inclusivity, I aimed to create a fun and engaging brand identity for Hack@Brown.

Problem

Since coding often targets a specific group and represents a stereotypical image, a more approachable and friendly brand identity was needed.
