SEQUENCE 2019

Reminiscing about the old memories each photographs hold is something I take time in my daily lives. Constantly, we are exposed to a method of collecting and storing parts of our memories either analogically or digitally. Inspired by the photographs that I took during this summer, I recreated my instagram posts into a 50 seconds video. I am in the process of editing minor shots of the video.

Design 1 and 2 capture my daily routine when I come home: washing hands and lighting up candles.
