Roomof

Objective

Enables housemates to split bills equally and conveniently from anywhere in the world

Role

Prototyping, UI Design, UX Design

Tools

Figma, Cinema 4D, Illustrator

Overview

This is a project created with Cognitive Psychology and UI/UX knowledge in mind.

Role

My roles included creating platforms for the mobile application. I conducted preliminary research to support the intervention and UI decisions such as user flow, microinteractions, and prototyping. I ensured that the interface provides a seamless experience to all target users.

Roomof

This application allows splitting the bills for rent, utilities, and shared household items with your housemates. The app will provide the functionality to make payments on time initiated by a platform like an Internet provider, electricity company, and etc.

Deliverable

How can I provide convenience and a sense of transparency in paying bills in a shared household?
