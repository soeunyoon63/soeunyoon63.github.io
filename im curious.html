WHY DO MOSQUITO BITES ITCH?

2019

I focused on the word choice when we say mosquitos "bite". In fact, mosquitos use part of their mouthes to suck blood through our skin. I found some of the common words that are usually misused. I merged the wrong and correct definitions in the illustrations and created into postcards that children can have easy access to learning.
