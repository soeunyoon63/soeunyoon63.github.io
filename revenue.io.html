Overview

Revenue.io is a complete AI-powered RevOps platform that delivers real-time guidance in the moments that matter.

Rebrand Deliverables

When I joined the team, Revenue.io (formerly known as ringDNA) decided to do a rebranding. I worked closely with the design team to retain the existing ringDNA visual system while expanding its expression to reimagine concepts around revenue.
* full brand guidelines available on request

Task

I collaborated with the marketing team to create engaging content aimed at generating leads and establishing our brand as trustworthy and relevant. My design work included ads, blogs, podcasts, ebooks, infographics, and marketing campaigns. Throughout this process, I balanced creative freedom with specific requests from stakeholders.
As part of the brand exploration, I continuously developed a collection of 3D icons, illustrations, and shapes for various applications. I took ownership of internal assets, brand guidelines, and templates for marketing content, ensuring they aligned with our rebranding efforts. Additionally, I updated our previous content to reflect the new brand identity.
