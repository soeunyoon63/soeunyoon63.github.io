Overview

Revenue.io is a complete AI-powered RevOps platform that delivers real-time guidance in the moments that matter.

Rebrand Deliverables

When I joined the team, Revenue.io (formerly known as ringDNA) decided to do a rebrand. I worked closely with the design team to retain the existing ringDNA visual system and expanded the expression to re-imagine concepts around revenue.
* full brand guidelines available on request

Task

I collaborated with the marketing team to execute engaging content to help find leads and establish our brand as trustworthy and relevant. The design work includes ads, blogs, podcasts, ebooks, infographics, and marketing campaigns. Balancing creative freedom within some defined requests from the stakeholders is what I kept in mind.
I explored and contributed to the brand exploration by continuously creating a pack of 3D icons, illustrations, and shapes for multiple uses. I took ownership of internal assets, brand guidelines, and templates for marketing content. I continued to update our previous content to our rebrand.
