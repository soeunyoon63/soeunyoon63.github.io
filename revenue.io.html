Rebrand Deliverables

When I joined the team, Revenue.io (formerly known as ringDNA) decided to do a rebrand. I worked closely with the design team to retain the exisitng ringDNA visual system and expand the expression to re-imagine concepts around revenue. The rebrand was successful launched after 8-month of work of positioning strategic direction of the company and new core values.
