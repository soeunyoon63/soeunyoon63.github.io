CHEEZ IT 2019

Analyzing different styles of artists, I recreated the covers of Cheez It crackers. I broke down core elements of style including typeface, shapes, image treatment and rule lines. Out of 10 style studies, I chose 3 designs and developed further into a real-life size mockups.
