COLOR THEORY

2019

Focusing on the combination of colors, these designs were created with untraditional method of using 3D rendering software to 2D project in the form of a booklet. Each design is printed on vinyl paper to give a sense of layering and outlining. The practice of making different color palette for each design can be seen. Multiple unexplored colors are incorporated throughout the color project.
