WORK ABOUT CONTACT

COLOR THEORY

2019

Print Design, Color

Each design is printed on vinyl paper to give a sense of layering and outlining. The practice of making different color palette for each design can be found. Specific color palettes are incorporated throughout this project. Focusing on the combination of colors, these designs were created with a new approach of using Rhinoceros, 3D rendering software to a 2D project in the form of a booklet.
