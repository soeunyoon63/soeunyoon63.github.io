To Forget List

Coding, Product Design

Ongoing

There is a common term used in Korea called “이불 킥” which literally means kick blankets. It is used for a situation when someone is going to sleep and suddenly remembers an embarrassing moment then he or she kicks the blanket unconsciously due to shame or regret. To Forget List mimics the desire of erasing and getting rid of certain memories.

Software Used

Adobe Creative Suite

Rhino 6

Experiment

The particles creates a visual system of a conceptual idea of trying to forget a section of a memory. According to the article, scientists had found brain that is transparent as Jell-O so networks of neurons that receive and send information can be highlighted in color and viewed in three-dimensional complexity without slicing up the organ. Inspired by the word "brain that is transparent as Jello-O", the content inside works as a storage for human memory systems. Similar to computer hardrives, human brain acts as a 'wet-drive' that is capable of capturing an array of memories.
