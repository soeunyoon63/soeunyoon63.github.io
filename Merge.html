Merge

Objective

Launching a new product with a functional package by understanding the material

Role

Package design, Print

Tools

Cinema 4D, Illustrator

Overview

With six signature scents in the candle collection, you can design your own unique blend by pairing two scents together to create a layered fragrance at home. The semi–circle glass candle comes together in a package, designed to be lit simultaneously. Merge provides a sense of personalization and luxury, opposed to grabbing a candle straight off the shelf.

Product Concept

There are loads of great scented products on the market, but who wants to smell like everyone else? Imagine a one–of–a–kind scent that has been designed based on your favorite fragrance and home environment. Suddenly that off–the–shelf candle doesn’t seem as appealing. Unique, bespoke scents often with a high price tag. Fortunately, Merge offers two scents at the price of one. The price range is $20–30.

Design Rationale

Having the corrugated side of the cardboard inside protects the glass candles, and holds matchsticks in place. The simple, yet modern design targets people who are interested in visually pleasing and personalized products.
