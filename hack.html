HACK@BROWN2020

2019

Hack@Brown is an annual hackathon run by students at Brown University. Working with non-designers and designers, a brand identity for Hack@Brown2020 is created with using a 3D renderer, Cinema4D, and new material texture. Creating publicity section of the page which include Facebook banner, workshop banners was one of the tasks. This year theme is Garage.
